Pig Game

Game Rules 📑

An interactive two-player dice game where players take turns rolling a dice. Players accumulate points but lose their turn and points for the round if they roll a one. The game continues until one player reaches 100 points. Click '✅ Start game' to begin and '🔄 New game' to restart. Players can change their username if they want before and during the play as well just by clicking over the current player username and typing a new one.
